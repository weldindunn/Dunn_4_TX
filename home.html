Our Mission

Weldin Dunn

Democrat for Houston

I couldn't be prouder to be a Houstonian. We are energy capital of the world, a leader in trade and healthcare, one of the two most important spaceports, and the most economically thriving city in the United States.

Bringing Houston values to Washington is a privilege I hold close to heart. We would truly be a stronger nation if everyone held the good-neighbor principles we do. If there is anything I can do to improve your life, I will seek out a solution.
